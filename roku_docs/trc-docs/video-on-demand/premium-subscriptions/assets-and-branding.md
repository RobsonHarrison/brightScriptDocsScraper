## Planners
We will need updates to your title trackers (planners) by the first of the month, for the following month (i.e. Jan 1, we would like to see your February title trackers). On-time updates allow us to incorporate these updates into The Roku Channel, Premium Subscriptions editorial, and marketing plans.
Email svod-editorial@roku.com with a spreadsheet listing all titles and availability windows.
Follow the template – found [HERE](https://devtools.web.roku.com/samples/SVOD%20Planner%20Template.xlsx)
Your spreadsheet should highlight any “high value” content. Originals, featured titles, etc. will be ushered through the process.
This is not the place to communicate takedowns or changed availability dates (MDUs). This is **for reference only**. Updates to your metadata should go through our Content Operations team.
